# 🎮 COSMIC QUEST - QUICK START GUIDE

## 🚀 Start Playing in 30 Seconds

### Step 1: Open in Browser
Simply open **`index.html`** in your web browser - that's it! No installation needed.

### Step 2: Choose Your Action
Click the buttons to play:
- 🔍 **EXPLORE** - Enter a new room (monster, treasure, or empty)
- ⚔️ **FIGHT** - Attack an enemy (appears only in battle)
- 🏃 **RUN** - Escape from combat (risky!)
- ❌ **QUIT** - Exit the game

### Step 3: Defeat Enemies & Collect Treasure!
- **Gain XP** from defeating monsters
- **Level Up** to increase your damage
- **Collect Gold** from treasure and fallen foes
- **Defeat Bosses** for massive rewards

## 🎮 Game Mechanics

### Combat
1. Enemy appears → click **⚔️ FIGHT**
2. You attack → enemy takes damage
3. Enemy counter-attacks → you take damage
4. Repeat until someone wins
5. Victory = XP + Gold!

### Escaping
- Click **🏃 RUN** during battle
- Higher level = better escape chance
- But you don't get rewards!

### Leveling Up
- Gain XP from defeated enemies
- Level up automatically
- Damage increases each level
- XP threshold increases per level

### Encounters
- **50% Monsters** - Combat, XP, loot
- **25% Treasure** - Gold coins
- **15% Empty Rooms** - Safe, no rewards
- **10% Bosses** - Rare, powerful, huge rewards

---

## 🏆 Victory Conditions

**Win:** Escape the dungeon alive
- Click **❌ QUIT** when you're satisfied
- Keep all your gold and achievements

**Lose:** Your health reaches 0
- Enemy defeats you
- Game over, stats shown

## 💡 Strategy Tips

### Easy Mode
- Explore cautiously
- Build up XP before fighting bosses
- Run from dangerous enemies

### Hard Mode
- Fight every enemy
- Seek out bosses
- Risk health for high rewards

### Balanced
- Mix fighting and exploring
- Find treasure to heal indirectly (quit with more)
- Manage your risk carefully

## ❓ Troubleshooting

**Page looks blank?**
- Make sure you're viewing `index.html`, not another file
- Try a different browser

**Buttons don't work?**
- Open browser console (F12) to check for errors
- Refresh the page

**Want to restart?**
- Click the 🔄 PLAY AGAIN button in game over screen
- Or refresh the page
